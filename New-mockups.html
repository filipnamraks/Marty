The agenda is the artifact

An agenda comes in (pasted, or fetched via a local MCP from a one-line instruction). Its headlines and subheadlines become a living document. While you talk, the LLM fills each section from the transcript; afterward it refines the notes into clean bullet points. The transcript is demoted to a collapsible peek. Ledger — the rules. Agenda intake by paste or one-line MCP fetch · headlines/subheadlines become the document skeleton · per-section status (filled ✓ / writing now ● / upcoming) · single scrolling document is the whole screen, no tabs · raw transcript lives in a collapsible peek at the bottom · a refine pass after the meeting turns rough notes into clean bullets · the finished agenda IS the deliverable.

Decide before building: how to treat agenda items that weren't discussed (leave empty vs. "not covered"), and where off-agenda topics go (a parking-lot / off-agenda bucket).
